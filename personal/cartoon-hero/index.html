Clutch the Hawk

About Clutch

Clutch the Hawk is an American mountain hawk that lives in the hills of the Lehigh Valley of Pennsylvania and is the official mascot of Lehigh University. He enjoys dancing at football games and gnawing on hares and campus squirrels.

Clutch enjoys participating in academic activities, cheering at football games, and soaring among the skies on the campus of Lehigh University

Characteristics

Clutch is the epitome of spirit of the Lehigh Valley plays hard and works just as hard. He believes in the team over individualistic pursuits

  1. Leadership
  2. Character
  3. Athletics

Friends

His friends are some of the other collegiate mascots in the Patriot League Athletic Conference

  • Rhett the Terrier (Boston)
  • The Crusader (Holy Cross)
  • The Raider (Colgate)
  • Bucky the Bison (Bucknell)
